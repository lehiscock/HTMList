ABOUT THIS WEBSITE:

HTMList aims to address HTML web design from an Information Science perspective. When creating a website, it’s crucial to consider any informational needs your user base may have. These questions or issues should be vital in shaping how your web page is structured.

This website offers an array of resources of coding in HTML, regardless of your level of expertise. These are meant to teach the fundamentals and methods, as well as different databases you can use to your advantage.

Dialogue is important in maintaining an adaptable environment in site creation. Updates to the site and information will be available through unscheduled status updates (in the “Posts” page). You can also contact the creator of this site via email: lehiscock@uncg.edu
